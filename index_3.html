About us

Welcome to Vintage Velvet, where culinary artistry meets timeless elegance. Vintage Velvet is renowned for its exquisite blend of traditional flavors and contemporary presentation. Since 1964, we have delighted patrons with our commitment to using only the freshest ingredients sourced from local farmers and markets. Our menu, curated by esteemed chefs with a passion for innovation, showcases a symphony of flavors that pays homage to both classic dishes and bold, inventive creations. Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a celebratory gathering, or a casual lunch, our warm hospitality and refined ambiance ensure a memorable dining experience. At Vintage Velvet, we invite you to savor the essence of culinary excellence in every bite, surrounded by the timeless allure of our distinguished establishment.
